# EduPlatform — Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** July 21, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how EduPlatform ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our mobile application and website (the "Service").

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Account information
- **Email address** and/or **phone number** — used for authentication via one-time passwords (OTP).
- **Display name** — optional, shown to other users in your role context.
- **Role** — Student, Teacher, or Admin (chosen at first login; Admin can reassign).

### 1.2 Usage data
- Lessons completed, test scores, Q&A posts, live class attendance.
- IP address and user-agent (for security audit logs).
- Approximate device type (mobile / desktop / tablet).

### 1.3 Content you create
- Questions and answers you post in Q&A.
- Live class chat messages (transient; not stored permanently).
- Submissions to tests and exams.

### 1.4 We DO NOT collect
- Precise geolocation.
- Contacts, photos, or media files.
- Biometric data.
- Browsing history outside the app.

## 2. How We Use Your Information

- **Authenticate** you and keep your account secure.
- **Deliver** lessons, tests, and live classes.
- **Personalize** your dashboard with progress tracking.
- **Detect and prevent** abuse, fraud, and unauthorized access.
- **Comply** with legal obligations and respond to lawful requests.

## 3. Legal Basis (GDPR)

For users in the European Economic Area:
- **Performance of a contract** — providing the educational service you requested.
- **Legitimate interest** — security logging and abuse prevention.
- **Consent** — for any optional analytics (you can withdraw anytime).

## 4. Data Sharing

We do **not** sell your data. We share data only with:
- **Cloud infrastructure providers** (AWS) hosting the Service.
- **Email/SMS delivery providers** (Resend, Twilio/SNS) for OTP delivery.
- **AI question-generation providers** (Groq) — only when an Admin explicitly triggers AI generation; no student data is sent.
- **Law enforcement** when legally required.

## 5. Data Retention

- Active account data: retained while your account is active.
- Inactive accounts: deleted or anonymized after 24 months of inactivity.
- Audit logs: retained for 12 months.
- OTP codes: deleted after 24 hours.

## 6. Your Rights

You have the right to:
- **Access** the personal data we hold about you.
- **Correct** inaccurate data.
- **Delete** your account and associated data.
- **Export** your data in a machine-readable format.
- **Object** to processing based on legitimate interests.
- **Withdraw consent** for optional processing.

To exercise these rights, email **privacy@eduplatform.app**.

## 7. Security

- All passwords/OTPs are stored as salted cryptographic hashes.
- All network traffic uses HTTPS/TLS 1.2+.
- Database access is restricted to authenticated application roles.
- Regular security audits and penetration testing.
- Parameterized queries (no SQL injection).
- Content Security Policy, X-Frame-Options, and HSTS enforced.

## 8. Children's Privacy

The Service is directed at educational institutions and individual learners aged 13 and above. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If you believe we have collected data from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

## 9. International Data Transfers

Your data may be processed in countries other than your own (e.g., AWS regions). We rely on Standard Contractual Clauses and adequacy decisions for such transfers.

## 10. Cookies

We use only essential cookies:
- `ep_sid` — your authenticated session token (httpOnly, secure).
- No tracking, advertising, or analytics cookies.

## 11. Changes to This Policy

We will notify you of material changes via email and/or in-app notification at least 30 days before they take effect.
